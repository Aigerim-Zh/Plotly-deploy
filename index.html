1,458 Bacteria Species 'New to Science' Found in Our Belly Buttons

In 2012, a group of North Carolina-based researchers in their new Belly Button Biodiversity (BBB) project published results of their first of many experiments.
They swabbed 60 belly buttons and identified a total of 2,368 species of bacteria. People's individual bacteria profiles were unique.
Some bacteria may be new to the science and some are completely unknow.
